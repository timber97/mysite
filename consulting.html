Consulting

I help teams think clearly, plan under uncertainty, and run high-consequence operations. My experience: seven years on strategic teams across government and tech; currently a Director at the Protocol Institute.

Facilitation & strategic planning

Working sessions that actually work. Offsites, strategic plans, priority-setting, alignment across functions. I’ve done this for multiple government executive teams, and I treat facilitation as design work, not moderation.

Good fit if your team: keeps meeting without deciding anything, chases too many balls at once, or needs to create legibility for stakeholders.

Safety & resilience reviews

Operational reviews grounded in modern safety science rather than checklist compliance. I designed an occupational health and safety system for an org of over 5,500 employees. Suited to high-consequence work: mining, aviation, public relations, remote camps, health, emergency management.

Good fit if your team: wants to understand how high performance works, not just log incidents.

Org & program design

Stand up new programs, fix broken ones, and run clean transitions. Mergers, reorgs, wind-downs, leadership handoffs, grant and fiscal setup. I’ve launched a ~$25M research institute, run a research program on a $1M annual budget, and led a government business-transformation initiative.

Good fit if your organization is: launching, growing, restructuring, or closing something and needs it done without loose ends.

How I work

Engagements are scoped to a defined outcome. I’m comfortable with a one-off session or a longer piece of work. Based in Whitehorse, but I work on-site, remotely, or on the road for the right engagement. I always like to start with a short call to see if it’s a fit.
